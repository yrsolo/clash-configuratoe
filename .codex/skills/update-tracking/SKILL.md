# Skill: update-tracking

## Purpose

Keep `work/` useful without turning it into ceremony.

## Use When

- the task is larger than a local tweak
- there is a stage boundary worth recording
- evidence or residual risks should be preserved

## Method

1. Update `work/now/current-task.md`.
2. Update `work/now/plan.md` when the scope changes.
3. Record checks, verified outcomes, and residual risks in `work/now/evidence.md`.
